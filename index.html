Instructions:

A classic two-person game. Players start each round by saying, “rock, paper, scissors, shoot!” On “shoot,” each player presses either r for rock, p for paper, or s for scissors. Rock crushes scissors, scissors cut paper, and paper covers rock. See who wins each round, Best out of 3!

    Press r for rock
    Press p for paper
    Press s for scissor

Press r, p or s to start playing!
